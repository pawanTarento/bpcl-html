BPMARRK 2.0

BPMARRK® is a patented technology developed by Corporate R&D Centre of Bharat Petroleum Corporation Ltd., for predicting detailed refining characteristics of crude oil within an hour. Refining characteristics of crude oil consist of key information such as TBP distillation yields, detailed cutwise distillates and residues qualities, hydrogen consumption in hydroprocessing, Fluid Catalytic Cracking Unit (FCCU) yields, Delayed Coker Unit (DCU) yields and selection of crude oil/blends for diverse business decisions.

BPMARRK® indeed helps in generating quality data, reducing human efforts, chemical/solvents consumptions in laboratory and time from one month to one hour for detailed crude oil characterizations. BPMARRK® output is useful for Process Engineers, Supply Chain Optimization and International Trades for advance planning, oil selection, real-time monitoring, controlling and optimization of Refinery Operations.
